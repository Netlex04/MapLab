MapLab

A precision-first design language for collaborative ECU tuning. Dark, technical, and built for engineers who demand accuracy.

Stack: Next.js 15 · Tailwind · shadcn/ui  ·  Theme: Dark Only  ·  Version: 1.0.0
00

Design Philosophy

Precision

Every pixel earns its place. No decorative noise — only information that matters. Like a well-calibrated ECU, nothing is wasted.

Transparency

Raw values, visible diffs, honest status. The UI never hides complexity — it makes complexity legible.

Authority

Dark and focused. The aesthetic of diagnostic equipment and professional tooling. Not a consumer app — a craftsman's workbench.
